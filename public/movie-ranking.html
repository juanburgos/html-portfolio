The Best Movies According to Juan

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Well, maybe not all-time best but 3 really good movies that will touch your heart.

Make sure you have ice cream in-stock.

Maquia: When the Promised Flower Blooms

Fantastic story and animation. With a satisfying resolution that will leave you in tears.

When Marnie Was There

Beautiful plot full of knots that unravels at the end. One of those movies that you can watch over and over.

Song of the Sea

Great multi-cultural animation and outstanding soundtrack. Funny and more family oriented. Another beautiful story that will leave you craving for more sweets and your grandma's hugs and kisses.
